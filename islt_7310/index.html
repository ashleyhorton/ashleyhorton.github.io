About Me

I have been an elementary educator for the past nine years. I have experience teaching grades third through sixth as well as summer school experience with early elementary. I have a Master of Arts degree in K-12 School Administration and currently working on my Educational Specialist in Learning Technologies and Design. My long-term goal is to work as an instructional designer, preferably for a curriculum company or university. My top five strengths are communication, significance, empathy, strategic, and futuristic.
