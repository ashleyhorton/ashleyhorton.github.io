Personal Statement

I have, without a doubt, truly enjoyed the Learning Technologies and Design program at the University of Missouri. I love that my courses were completely different from my Masters degree in School Administration; whereas my Masters degree involved writing paper after paper, my Specialist degree was hands-on and involved designing and developing projects that were applicable to my life and career.

I developed and demonstrated skills in web design and coding, front-end analysis, project management, game design, analytics, and development tools. Ultimately, my goal was to work for LAUNCH with Springfield Public Schools as an instructional designer. I am now utilizing my competencies as a digital learning developer. I work closely with course writers to develop the virtual courses students across the state of Missouri access. With the skills I have learned in the Learning Technologies and Design program, I am able to successfully develop quality courses.
